Background Info

In the first three installments, the only playable protagonist is Kazuma Kiryu. However, Yakuza 4 introduces multiple playable protagonists, which include Kazuma Kiryu, Masayoshi Tanimura, Shun Akiyama, and Taiga Saejima.

Yakuza 5 continued this trend and featured five playable protagonists; Kazuma Kiryu, Shun Akiyama, Taiga Saejima, Tatsuo Shinada, as well as the long running supporting character, Haruka Sawamura.[4] The prequel installment featured two protagonists, including another long running supporting character Goro Majima, alongside Kazuma Kiryu. Majima will be playable again in the remake of Yakuza 2, Ryu Ga Gotoku Kiwami 2.

The latest mainline installment, Shin Ryu Ga Gotoku will feature a new main character, Kasuga Ichiban
